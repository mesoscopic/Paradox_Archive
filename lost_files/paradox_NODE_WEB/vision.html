Using Paradox Vision

Paradox Vision is a Chromium extension designed by the Paradox team to help you narrow down your target audience and see how Paradox could help you advertise. By providing information about a product and its userbase, Vision can highlight opportunities to grow your audience wherever you browse. Our internal studies have shown it to give users up to a 32% boost in sales.

Download Paradox Vision

How does it work?

The internet is full of ads. Most websites use a common advertising framework that is compatible with Paradox. The Paradox Vision extension will scan websites for existing ad slots and check metadata about the website to determine if it might be appropriate for your target audience. You can fine-tune this by accepting and rejecting suggestions - this is all built on cutting-edge AI technology that responds and adjusts to your business' specific needs.

How do I install the extension?

Unfortunately, Paradox Vision is not available on a typical extension store due to security concerns. You will have to install it using WebCRX, a free and secure extension that is available on the extension store. We hope you will understand this decision, despite the slightly more complex installation method it requires. Enjoy!
